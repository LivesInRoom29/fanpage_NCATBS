Nick Cave & the Bad Seeds

Bio

In 1983, from the ashes of The Birthday Party, Nick Cave & the Bad Seeds would rise in a violent blaze of post-punk magnificence... and still they burn, with an ever-evolving, always enlightening reflection of humanity. Narratives in their songs range from the deepest darkest depths of the most sinister of psychopaths (not for the faint of heart) to, in their own way, the purest forms of love and devotion.

The work of Nick Cave and the Bad Seeds has been influntial for many other artists. Those that have covered their songs include Johnny Cash, Metallica, PJ Harvey, Cat Power, and the Arctic Monkeys... along with many, many others.
